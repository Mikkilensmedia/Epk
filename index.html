About Mikki

Mikki Lens is a visionary Afrobeat artist revolutionizing the genre with a fusion of traditional West African rhythms and contemporary production. With roots in Benue though based in Abuja and a global perspective, Mikki's music celebrates cultural heritage while pushing artistic boundaries.

A multi-talented songwriter and performer, Mikki has captivated audiences across continents with electrifying live shows and chart-topping releases. Drawing inspiration from legends while carving their own path, Mikki Lens represents the new generation of Afrobeat innovation.

With millions of streams and collaborations with international producers, Mikki continues to elevate Afrobeat on the global stage, making music that moves bodies and touches hearts.
